Multicart [source]

Multicart is a project built around PyGame containing a variety of different game concepts as scenes inside of a larger project. Reminiscent of 72-in-1 style games of the 80s and 90s. By standardizing the game scenes, we can easily add new game concepts to the project without having to reinvent the wheel each time we want to play with an idea.

Multicart looks to be the Django of PyGame development with a batteries included approach to rapid game design and iteration.
- Highwind

Sonic Blaster [source] [install]

Sonic Blaster is a Discord bot that provides Text-to-Speech functionality for Discord servers. Have no microphone but wish to communicate over voice? Sonic Blaster's Text-to-Speech can help.

Sonic Blaster is a great tool for those who wish to communicate over voice but lack a microphone.
